Get Started

Join the Bear Strength team to push your limits and grow alongside a community that values dedication, resilience, and raw power. At Bear Strength, we believe in empowering individuals to achieve their full potential through hard work, commitment, and relentless pursuit of strength. Whether you’re an experienced lifter or just starting out, you’ll be part of a supportive environment that celebrates progress, fosters camaraderie, and encourages personal growth. Together, we’ll break barriers, set new personal records, and embody the spirit of true strength. Step up, challenge yourself, and be a part of something powerful.
Here are our services

Personalized Training Programs

I will design tailored powerlifting routines based on your goals, experience, and equipment availability, ensuring progressive strength gains and optimal performance improvement.

Technique Analysis

Through video assessments, I’ll provide detailed feedback on your form for each lift, helping you avoid injury and improve technique for maximum efficiency and power output.

Nutrition Guidance

Receive personalized nutrition advice and macro adjustments to support your strength goals, ensuring you’re fueling your body properly for recovery, muscle growth, and optimal performance.

Weekly Progress Check-ins

We’ll review your progress weekly with in-depth discussions on your lifts, challenges, and adjustments needed to keep you on track towards your goals.
